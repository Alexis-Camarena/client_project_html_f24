Student Success Portal

Welcome to the UM Grad Success Navigator

This website is set up to help you be successul inside and outside of the classroom and we have included the following resources throughout our site:

Academic Resources:

Access study guides, tutoring services, library resources, and tips on time management to excel in your coursework.

Physical Health:

Find information on gym memberships, fitness classes, and nutritional advice to maintain your physical well-being.

Mental Health:

Explore counseling services, stress management workshops, and mindfulness exercises to support your mental health.

Life Outside the Classroom:

Discover clubs, social events, and networking opportunities that enhance your graduate experience.

Contact:

Reach out to our support team for personalized guidance or to answer any questions you might have.

Career Development

Prepare for your career post-graduation with resources from our Career Development Center. We offer resume reviews, interview preparation, and connections with industry professionals.

Job Boards:

Access exclusive job listings for graduate students.

Workshops:

Attend workshops on resume building, networking, and job search strategies.

Mentorship:

Connect with alumni mentors who can provide guidance and support in your field.

Financial Aid and Scholarships

Explore various funding options available to support your graduate studies. Our financial aid office provides resources on scholarships, grants, and loans tailored for graduate students.

Scholarship Opportunities:

Discover scholarships specific to your program or background.

Grant Writing Assistance:

Get help with writing grant proposals to fund your research projects.

Budgeting Tips:

Learn how to manage your finances effectively during your studies.

Research and Innovation

Engage in groundbreaking research and innovation at UM. Explore opportunities to collaborate with faculty, participate in research projects, and present your work at conferences.

Research Grants:

Apply for funding to support your research endeavors.

Lab Resources:

Access state-of-the-art labs and equipment for your projects.

Innovation Hub:

Connect with the UM Innovation Hub to explore startup resources and entrepreneurial initiatives.

International Student Support

If you are an international student, find support services tailored to your needs. We offer visa guidance, cultural integration programs, and language support.

Visa Assistance:

Get help with maintaining your student visa status and understanding work regulations.

Cultural Integration:

Participate in programs designed to help you adjust to life in the U.S. and connect with other international students.

Language Resources:

Access language support services including tutoring and conversation groups.

Student Life at the University of Michigan

Life Outside the Classroom

Graduate school is not just about academics; it's about building a balanced life and making the most of your time at UM. Here are some ways to engage and thrive outside the classroom:

Student Organizations

Explore a wide range of student organizations and get involved in campus life:

  • Cultural Groups: Join groups celebrating diverse cultures and traditions.
  • Academic Groups: Connect with academic societies and research-focused organizations.
  • Professional Groups: Network with peers in your field and attend industry-related events.
  • Social Groups: Engage in social activities and hobbies with fellow students.

Arts and Culture

Immerse yourself in the vibrant arts and cultural scene at UM:

  • University Arts Initiative: Discover cultural events, exhibitions, and performances on campus.
  • University of Michigan Museum of Art (UMMA): Explore diverse art collections and exhibitions.
  • School of Music, Theatre & Dance: Attend student and faculty performances in music, theatre, and dance.

Career Services

Prepare for your future career with these resources:

  • Career Center: Access career counseling, job search resources, and workshops.
  • University Employment Opportunities: Explore job openings and internships at UM.

Community Engagement

Make a difference through community service and volunteering:

  • Community Service Programs: Find local volunteering opportunities and service projects.
  • Rotary Club of Ann Arbor: Get involved in local service initiatives and community projects.
  • Volunteer Ann Arbor: Explore various volunteer opportunities in the Ann Arbor area.

Local Resources

Navigate life in Ann Arbor with these local resources:

  • Explore Ann Arbor: Discover local attractions, dining, and shopping.
  • Yelp Ann Arbor: Find reviews and recommendations for local businesses and services.
  • Ann Arbor News: Stay updated on local news and events.
